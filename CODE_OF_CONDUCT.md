# Code of Conduct

This project follows the Contributor Covenant.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- being respectful and precise
- accepting constructive feedback
- focusing on the project goals and technical facts
- showing empathy for other contributors

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment or abusive language
- personal attacks
- publishing private information without consent
- intentionally disruptive or bad-faith behavior

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards.

## Reporting

Report conduct issues to `info@malzi.space`.
